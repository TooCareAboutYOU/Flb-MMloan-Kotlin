FAQ:

T: What are the requirements to apply for a loan?

We only need you to be 18 years old, own and ID, and have a stable job.

T: How to apply for a loan?

Open Google Play, search app “MMloan”, and download the app. After you finished installing the app, open the app, and press the button “Borrow Now”, and you can start on the application process.

T: How is the credit rating process?

Our system automatically does the credit rating based on the information that you have filled in. Please make sure that the information is true, if the information you filled in does not meet our requirements, the system will decide to reject your application. If we found any false information filled in, we might report it to the credit bureau, and it might affect your credit record.

T: How to repay the loan? Can we repay the loan in advance?

Our reminder team will call your mobile one day before the due date and on the due date to remind you to repay the loan on time. You can repay the loan through 7-11 and other stores, you can check out the available stores on the repayment page. We allow advance payment; however, we will not reduce the interest.

T: What if my loan is overdue? How to calculate late penalty?

If your loan is overdue, our collection team will contact you and carry on the collection procedure. The late penalty is 2%/day.

T: Why was my application rejected? Can I apply for another loan?

If your loan application was rejected, it means that your credit scoring did not meet the requirement. The system will carry on big data analyzation and verify loan applications automatically. To protect our users’ privacy, we are not able to disclose the detailed reason for the result. After your application was rejected, you can apply for another loan in 7 days.

T: Is the data I give to MMloan safe?

We promise that we will not disclose our users’ data to a third party without our users’ consent (unless it is needed for collection and carrying on our services).
